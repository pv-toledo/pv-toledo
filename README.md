# Paulo Vinícius Vieira Toledo

**Industrial Process Engineer · MSc Metallurgical Engineering**

I work at the intersection of steelmaking process expertise and data-driven methods. My background is in thermodynamic and kinetic modeling of steel refining — steel/slag interactions, desulfurization mechanisms, high-temperature phase equilibria — and I have been building applied machine learning on top of that foundation to solve real industrial process problems.

Currently based in China, working in large-scale industrial production at CSN. Previously at ArcelorMittal Resende.
